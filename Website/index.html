How to use this website

Counter-19 sensors use our highly accurate people counting technology to anonymously count people as they enter and exit an area. When the customizable occupancy limit is met, connected display signage updates in real-time to alert and notify building users that the area is at capacity and not safe to enter. Counter-19 works independently of existing IT networks and is suitable for buildings and rooms of all sizes, even those with multiple entrances & exits.
